Lost & Found

[Place for lost & found office image]

Report or recover lost items at MACL Airport. Our team is here to help you reunite with your belongings quickly and securely.

  • Visit the Lost & Found office near Arrivals, open 24/7.
  • Call us at +233-123-456-789.
  • Email: lostfound@maclairport.com

Claiming Found Items

Bring a valid ID and proof of ownership. Items are held for 30 days. Unclaimed items are donated to charity. [Place for found items image]

Free Wi-Fi

[Place for Wi-Fi zone image]

Stay connected with complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout MACL Airport. Easy, fast, and secure access for all travelers.

  1. Select the "MACL Free WiFi" network on your device.
  2. Open your browser and follow the on-screen instructions.
  3. Enjoy unlimited browsing during your stay.

For assistance, visit our Information Desks or call +233-123-456-789. [Place for info desk image]

Accessibility Services

[Place for accessibility ramp image]

MACL Airport is committed to providing accessible facilities and services for all travelers. Assistance is available throughout your journey.

  • Wheelchair assistance available at all terminals
  • Accessible restrooms and elevators
  • Priority boarding and check-in
  • Dedicated parking spaces close to entrances

Contact our team for special assistance: +233-123-456-789 or access@maclairport.com [Place for staff assistance image]
